ICycleFinderFind Method (IAtomContainer, Int32, Int32)

Find the cycles of the provided molecule when an adjacent relation (graph) is already available. The graph can be obtained through ToAdjList(IAtomContainer), for convenience Find(IAtomContainer, Int32) will automatically create the graph.
