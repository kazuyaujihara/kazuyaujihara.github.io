InChINumbersToolsGetUSmilesNumbers Method

Obtain the InChI numbers for the input container to be used to order atoms in Universal SMILES O’Boyle, Noel, Towards a Universal SMILES representation - A standard method to generate canonical SMILES based on the InChI, Journal of Cheminformatics, 2012, 4:?-? . The numbers are obtained using the fixedH and RecMet options of the InChI. All non-bridged hydrogens are labelled as 0.
