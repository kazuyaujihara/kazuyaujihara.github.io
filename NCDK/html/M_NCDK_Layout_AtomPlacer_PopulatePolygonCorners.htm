AtomPlacerPopulatePolygonCorners Method

Populates the corners of a polygon with atoms. Used to place atoms in a geometrically regular way around a ring center or another atom. If this is used to place the bonding partner of an atom (and not to draw a ring) we want to place the atoms such that those with highest "weight" are placed far-most away from the rest of the molecules. The "weight" mentioned here is calculated by a modified Morgan number algorithm.
