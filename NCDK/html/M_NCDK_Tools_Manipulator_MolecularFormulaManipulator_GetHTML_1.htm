MolecularFormulaManipulatorGetHTML Method (IMolecularFormula, Boolean, Boolean)

Returns the string representation of the molecule formula based on Hill System with numbers wrapped in <sub></sub> tags and the isotope of each Element in <sup></sup> tags and the total charge of IMolecularFormula in <sup></sup> tags. Useful for displaying formulae in Swing components or on the web.
