Atomic

Charged
Discriminate protonation states.
Chiral
Generate different hash codes for stereoisomers. The currently supported geometries are:
  • Tetrahedral
  • Double Bond
  • Cumulative Double Bonds
Depth
Specify the depth of the hash generator. Larger values discriminate more molecules.
Elemental
Discriminate elements.

Perturbed
Discriminate atoms experiencing uniform environments. This method uses MinimumEquivalentCyclicSet to break symmetry but depending on application one may need a more comprehensive method. Please refer to PerturbWith(EquivalentSetFinder) for further configuration details.
Radical
Discriminate free radicals.
SuppressHydrogens
Suppress any explicit hydrogens in the encoding of hash values. The generation of hashes acts as though the hydrogens are not present and as such preserves stereo-encoding.
