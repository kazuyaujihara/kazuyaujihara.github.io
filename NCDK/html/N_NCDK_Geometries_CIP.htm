Classes
  ClassDescription
CIPTool
Tool to help determine the R,S and stereochemistry definitions of a subset of the CIP rules Cahn, R.S. and Ingold, C. and Prelog, V., Specification of Molecular Chirality, Angew. Chem. Int. Ed., 1966, 5:385-415. The used set up sub rules are specified in the CIPLigandRule class.
ImplicitHydrogenLigand
Subclass of Ligand to which no further recursion must be applied.
LigancyFourChirality
Stereochemistry specification for quadrivalent atoms to be used for the CIP algorithm only.
Ligand
Concept of a ligand in CIP terms, reflecting a side chain of a central atom that can have precedence over another.
TerminalLigand
Subclass of Ligand to which no further recursion must be applied.
VisitedAtoms
Helper class for the CIPTool to keep track of which atoms have already been visited.
