VABCVolume
Calculates the Van der Waals volume using the method proposed in Zhao, Yuan H. and Abraham, Michael H. and Zissimos, Andreas M., Fast Calculation of van der Waals Volume as a Sum of Atomic and Bond Contributions and Its Application to Drug Compounds, The Journal of Organic Chemistry, 2003, 68:7368-7373 . The method is limited to molecules with the following elements: H, C, N, O, F, Cl, Br, I, P, S, As, B, Si, Se, and Te.
