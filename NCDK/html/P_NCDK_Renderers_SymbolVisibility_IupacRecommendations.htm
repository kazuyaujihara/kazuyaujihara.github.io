Displays a symbol based on the preferred representation from the IUPAC guidelines (GR-2.1.2) Jonathan Brecher, Graphical representation standards for chemical structure diagrams (IUPAC Recommendations 2008), Pure Appl. Chem, 2008, 80:277–410. Carbons are unlabeled unless they have abnormal valence, parallel bonds, or are terminal (i.e. methyl, methylene, etc).
