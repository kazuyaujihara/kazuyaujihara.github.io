MMElementRule Class

This class validate if the occurrence of the IElements in the IMolecularFormula, for metabolites, are into a maximal limit according paper: The study is from 2 different mass spectral databases and according different mass of the metabolites. The analysis don't take account if the IElement is not contained in the matrix. It will be jumped.
