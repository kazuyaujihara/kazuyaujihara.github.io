Remarks
The data model defines the central, chiral IAtom, and its four ILigands, each of which has an ligand IAtom, directly bonded to the chiral atom via an IBond. The ordering of the four ligands is important, and defines together with the Stereo to spatial geometry around the chiral atom. The first ligand points towards to observer, and the three other ligands point away from the observer; the Stereo then defines the order of the second, third, and fourth ligand to be clockwise or anti-clockwise.
