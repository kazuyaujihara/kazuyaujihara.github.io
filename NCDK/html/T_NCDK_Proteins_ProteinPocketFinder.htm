ProteinPocketFinder Class

The detection of pocket and cavities in a bioPolymer is done similar to the program LIGSITE Hendlich, M. and Rippmann, F. and Bernickel, G. , LIGSITE: Automatic and efficient detection of potential small molecule-binding sites in proteins, Journal of Molecular Graphics and Modelling, 1997, 15:359-363.
