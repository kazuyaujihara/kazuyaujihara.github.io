CDKRNode Class

Node of the resolution graphe (RGraph) An CDKRNode represents an association betwwen two edges of the source graphs G1 and G2 that are compared. Two edges may be associated if they have at least one common feature. The association is defined outside this class. The node keeps tracks of the ID of the mapped edges (in an CDKRMap), of its neighbours in the RGraph it belongs to and of the set of incompatible nodes (nodes that may not be along with this node in the same solution)
